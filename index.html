Our Services

Everything Your Outdoor Space Needs

From full backyard makeovers to detailed garden work — we've got you covered.

Full Backyard Landscaping

Complete backyard transformations from bare dirt to beautiful outdoor living spaces. We handle design, earthworks, planting, and finishing touches so you get a space that actually works for your lifestyle.

Front Yard & Streetscape Design

First impressions matter, especially in newer estates around Charlemont and Armstrong Creek. We create front yard designs that complement your home's style and stand out on the street.

Garden Bed Installation & Planting

From native Australian plants suited to Geelong's climate to structured garden beds with proper drainage and edging. We select plants that thrive locally and keep maintenance manageable year-round.

Turf Installation & Lawn Prep

Proper soil preparation, grading, and quality turf supply and installation for lawns that establish quickly. We make sure your new lawn is set up to handle Geelong's dry summers and wet winters.

Outdoor Structures & Hardscaping

Retaining walls, paving, garden edging, and other hardscape features that give your yard structure and function. Built to Australian standards with materials suited to local conditions.

FAQ

Common Questions, Straight Answers

We're based in Charlemont and service the wider Geelong region including Armstrong Creek, Grovedale, Waurn Ponds, Highton, Belmont, and surrounding suburbs. If you're unsure whether we cover your area, just give us a call and we'll let you know straight away.
Every yard is different, so pricing depends on the size of the space, the scope of work, and the materials involved. We provide a detailed, transparent quote after our on-site consultation so there are no surprises. We're happy to work within a range of budgets.
Smaller jobs like garden beds or turf installation can often be completed in a day or two. Larger backyard transformations typically take between one and two weeks depending on complexity. We'll give you a clear timeline upfront and we're known for sticking to it.
Most standard landscaping work doesn't require a permit, but certain structures like retaining walls over a specific height may need approval from the City of Greater Geelong. We'll let you know during the quoting process if any permits are required and can help guide you through the process.
